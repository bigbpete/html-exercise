Cities I have lived

In early years there was little choice in location (obviously). It was from Jacksonville forward where personal choices began. First a college degree and a commission in the Army follwed by work as a police officer in suburban Atlanta. Then came war in 2001; everything changed with my recall to active duty and the miriad of locations the Army took me afterwards. Then illness struck and a medical retirment from the Army took me to St. Augustine, Florida. From there work sent me on new adventures with the US Forest Service, then the US Air Force as a civilian.
